1-Retrieve the data in the title and description columns of the film table.
2-Retrieve all data in the film table where the film length (length) is greater than 60 and less than 75.
3-Retrieve all data in the film table where the rental_rate is 0.99 and the replacement_cost is either 12.99 or 28.99.
4-What is the last_name of the customer whose first_name is 'Mary' in the customer table?
5-Retrieve the data in the film table where the length is not greater than 50 and the rental_rate is neither 2.99 nor 4.99.

*Answer ; 
1 - SELECT title, description FROM film;
2 - SELECT * FROM film WHERE length > 60 AND length < 75;
3 - SELECT * FROM film WHERE rental_rate = 0.99 AND (replacement_cost = 12.99 OR replacement_cost = 28.99);
4 - SELECT last_name FROM customer WHERE first_name = 'Mary';
5 - SELECT * FROM film WHERE NOT (length > 50) AND NOT (rental_rate = 2.99 OR rental_rate = 4.99);
